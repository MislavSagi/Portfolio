About me

Hi, I’m Mislav a Croatian freelance web developer with a background in precision engineering and a strong focus on building clean, fast, and SEO-friendly websites that get results. Before transitioning into web development, I spent 5 years as a CNC operator and programmer, working on high-spec components for companies like Rimac Automobili and Lingl Solead GMBH. That experience taught me the value of precision, teamwork, and solving problems under pressure skills I bring into every web project. If you’re looking for a reliable partner to bring your website to life, I’d love to help you build something that reflects your vision and helps your business grow.
