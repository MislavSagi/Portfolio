FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What kind of projects do you work on?

I build digital experiences that are fast, sharp, and built to perform.

• Responsive websites that actually work on all screens — not just “look decent”
• E-commerce stores that load fast, convert better, and don’t break under pressure
• Custom web apps and slick, interactive UIs that make users want to stick around
• Portfolio sites and personal brands that leave a mark (and don’t look like a template)
• CMS builds on platforms like WordPress, Shopify, or whatever gets the job done right
• Website redesigns & performance upgrades that take your existing site from “meh” to whoa

How long does it take?

Typically, a basic website design and development can take anywhere from 1 to 3 weeks.
The timeline for each project varies depending on its complexity and scope. More complex projects, like e-commerce sites or custom web applications, may take 4 weeks or longer.

I always provide a detailed timeline during the project planning phase to ensure clear expectations and smooth delivery.

What’s your process like?

My process is designed to keep you involved and informed every step of the way. It typically includes:
• Discovery & Planning: Understanding your goals, target audience, and project requirements.
• Design: Creating wireframes and visual designs for your review and feedback.
• Development: Turning designs into a fully functional website with clean, efficient code.
• Testing: Ensuring your site works smoothly across all devices and browsers.
• Launch: Deploying the website live and making sure everything runs perfectly.
• Support: Offering ongoing maintenance and updates as needed.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing support packages that include updates, backups, performance checks, and minor content changes. I’m also available for one-time updates or feature additions as your site grows.

How much does a project cost?

Pricing depends on the project’s size and complexity. Basic websites typically start around $350–$600, with custom or e-commerce solutions ranging higher. I’ll provide a detailed quote after understanding your needs.
