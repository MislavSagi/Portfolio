Client: BioNaVis

Made With:

PROJECT GOAL: To design a creative, unique, and authentic website for my client's company and brand.
PROBLEMS: Too low or too high resolution images, content not fitting the design, balancing aesthetics and usability.
SOLUTIONS: - For the unfitting images, I used Canva to resize and change the aspect ratio so it could be aesthetically integrated in the design. - I requested an initial meeting with my client to talk about important topics such as their ideas, aspirations, and overall business dreams and goals, so I could use that as the foundation for the website's vibe and authenticity.

Client: PureTaste

Made With:

PROJECT: Website for Pure Taste Coffee Shop
PROJECT GOAL: The client requested a clean, minimalistic website design without animations. The primary challenge was to maintain simplicity while effectively conveying the authenticity and character of the coffee shop.
MY APPROACH: I collaborated closely with the client to understand their vision, preferences, and goals. Using this insight, I developed a custom, visually appealing design that reflects the brand’s identity and enhances its online presence.
